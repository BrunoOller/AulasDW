VERDANSK IS BACK

Verdansk is returning in CoD BO6 Season 3.

In the lead-up to Season 3, rumours that the map would be making a comeback had run rampant, with Activision finally confirming the news with a short trailer posted to social media on 10th March - the fifth anniversary of Call of Duty: Warzone.

SEASON 3

The Black Ops 6 and Warzone Season 3 release date is Thursday, April 03, 2025 at 9am PST / 12pm EST / 4pm GMT. This is a delay from the original release date, meaning Season 2 has been extended.

The original release date for Season 3 was March 20, 2025, when Season 2 was originally set to end. Activision confirmed the delay in an official tweet on March 3, claiming that the team is “taking the time to deliver a great experience.”
